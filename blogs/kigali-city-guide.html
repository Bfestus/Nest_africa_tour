Kigali: Africa's Cleanest Capital City

Discover why Kigali is considered one of Africa's most beautiful and cleanest capitals. From modern shopping centers to vibrant markets, excellent restaurants to important memorial sites, Kigali offers a perfect blend of history and progress.

Modern Kigali's Highlights

Shopping and Entertainment

  • Kigali City Tower
  • Kigali Convention Centre
  • The Kigali Heights complex
  • Modern shopping malls

Historical Sites

Important Memorial Sites

Understanding Rwanda's history through its memorial sites:

  • Kigali Genocide Memorial
  • Campaign Against Genocide Museum
  • Presidential Palace Museum

Local Markets and Culture

Vibrant Market Experience

  • Kimironko Market
  • Nyabugogo Market
  • Artisan craft centers
  • Local food markets

Dining Scene

Kigali's diverse culinary offerings:

  • Traditional Rwandan restaurants
  • International cuisine
  • Coffee shops and cafes
  • Rooftop dining venues

Green Initiatives

What makes Kigali Africa's cleanest city:

  • Monthly community cleaning day (Umuganda)
  • Plastic bag ban
  • Green spaces and parks
  • Environmental conservation efforts

Transportation

Getting around Kigali:

  • Taxi services
  • Motorcycle taxis (motos)
  • Public buses
  • Car rental services
