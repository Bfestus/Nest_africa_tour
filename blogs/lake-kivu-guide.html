Lake Kivu: Rwanda's Hidden Gem

Lake Kivu, one of Africa's Great Lakes, offers pristine beaches, water sports, and stunning sunsets. Explore the charming lakeside towns of Gisenyi and Kibuye, enjoy fresh fish cuisine, and experience the therapeutic hot springs.

Lakeside Towns

Gisenyi

The largest and most vibrant town on Lake Kivu's shores offers:

  • Public and private beaches
  • Water sports facilities
  • Luxury hotels and resorts
  • Waterfront restaurants

Kibuye

A picturesque town known for:

  • Stunning peninsula location
  • Island boat tours
  • Fresh fish markets
  • Peaceful atmosphere

Activities on Lake Kivu

Water Sports

  • Kayaking
  • Swimming
  • Boat tours
  • Fishing trips

Natural Attractions

Hot Springs

Visit the therapeutic hot springs known for their:

  • Healing properties
  • Natural warmth
  • Mineral content
  • Relaxation benefits

Local Cuisine

Enjoy fresh local delicacies:

  • Fresh tilapia
  • Grilled sambaza
  • Local fruits
  • Traditional dishes

Best Time to Visit

Lake Kivu can be visited year-round, but the best times are:

  • June to September (long dry season)
  • December to February (short dry season)

Plan Your Lake Kivu Adventure

Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, Lake Kivu offers something for everyone. Let us help you plan the perfect lakeside getaway.
