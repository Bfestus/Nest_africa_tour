About Nest Africa Travels Ltd

Nest Africa Travels Ltd is a dynamic Rwandan-based travel and tourism company dedicated to showcasing the best of Rwanda and beyond. We specialize in creating unforgettable travel experiences, helping clients explore Rwanda's rich culture, wonderful landscapes, vibrant environment, and warm communities, we make it easy for travelers to reach every destination they dream of visiting.

At Nest Africa, we believe in responsible tourism that benefits local communities while preserving Rwanda's natural beauty. Join us in creating unforgettable memories while making a positive impact on the places we visit.

Our Tours

Volcanoes National Park

Experience mountain gorilla trekking in their natural habitat. Home to endangered mountain gorillas and golden monkeys.

Akagera National Park

Safari adventure with the Big Five. Discover lions, elephants, buffalos, rhinos and leopards in Rwanda's only savanna park.

Nyungwe Forest

Canopy walk and chimpanzee tracking. Walk 50 meters above the forest floor and spot 13 primate species.

Lake Kivu

Lakeside relaxation and water sports. Enjoy pristine beaches, boat cruises, and stunning sunset views.

Mount Karisimbi Hiking

Experience the thrill of climbing Rwanda's highest peak. Challenge yourself with a 2-day trek to the summit at 4,507 meters above sea level.

Kigali City Tour

Explore Africa's cleanest capital. Visit genocide memorials, local markets, and experience modern Rwandan culture.
